Goals and Accomplishments

OUR GOALS

Brothers at Arms is a dedicated organization committed to unifying young black men and providing them with enhanced opportunities for personal growth. Our mission encompasses a wide range of initiatives, including but not limited to exposing these individuals to new hobbies, encouraging them to volunteer their time, and offering mentorship from older students. With a firm belief in the power of support systems, we strive to create a nurturing environment where these young men can flourish, empowering them to develop their spirits and broaden their life experiences. Our goal is to ensure that they have access to a myriad of opportunities, enabling them to thrive and reach their full potential.
