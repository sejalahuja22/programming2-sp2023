Gabriel VILUS is an immigrant hailing from the beautiful island country of Haiti. He is a junior attending Francis W. Parker School. He is a year round poet and athlete. His passions for poetry, Tennis, and Track and Field drive him to overcome any obstacles that come his way. DILLON MADKINS

Dillon Madkins is a Junior at Francis W. Parker high school. He is a year round athlete who participates in sports such as basketball, soccer, tennis, and Track and Field. He is very involved with comic books and video games and loves superheroes. His favorites are Spider-man, Nightwing, and Spawn. Dillon believes that an organization like this can hopefully provide guidance and assistance to young men who need it, especially young men of color who face the challenges that he does.
